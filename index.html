About Abdulrahman Al Shathry

Abdulrahman Al Shathry is a visionary leader and accomplished entrepreneur with over 26 years of experience as CEO of Saudi Controls Ltd., spearheading innovation and excellence in controls and engineering solutions. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering from the University of Southern California, and his career includes the successful merger forming Al-Shathry and Omrania (A&O), execution of over 200 projects in five years, and significant international business ventures.
