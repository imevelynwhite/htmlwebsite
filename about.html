About Abdulrahman Al Shathry

A visionary leader and accomplished entrepreneur with over 26 years of experience as CEO of Saudi Controls Ltd., spearheading innovation and excellence in controls and engineering solutions.

Under his leadership, the company has evolved into one of the region's foremost providers of smart automation, digital infrastructure, and sustainable engineering systems. Abdul Rahman Alshathry has played a pivotal role in advancing Saudi Arabia's transformation toward intelligent cities, efficient energy management, and Industry 4.0 readiness.

His approach combines deep technical expertise with a strategic vision for integrating local capabilities and global technologies, ensuring long-term growth and resilience.

Beyond his executive achievements, he is passionate about nurturing young talent, promoting research and development, and supporting initiatives that strengthen the Kingdom's knowledge-based economy.

His commitment to innovation, excellence, and national progress continues to inspire both his teams and the wider business community.
